Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Information We Collect

When you place an order with SV Jewels, we collect the following personal information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Shipping address

We also collect non-personal browsing data through Google Analytics and Google Ads (including cookies and usage data) to improve our website and serve relevant advertisements.

How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used solely to:

  • Process and fulfill your order
  • Send order confirmation and shipping updates
  • Respond to customer service inquiries
  • Comply with legal obligations

Payment Processing

All payments are processed securely by Whop. SV Jewels does not store, process, or have access to your payment card details. Please review Whop's privacy policy for details on their data practices.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share information with shipping carriers solely for the purpose of delivering your order.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies and tracking technologies through Google Analytics and Google Ads to analyze site traffic and measure advertising performance. You may opt out of Google Analytics tracking by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Data Retention

We retain order information for up to 3 years for record-keeping and customer service purposes, after which it is securely deleted.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at sterorx@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, email us at sterorx@gmail.com.
